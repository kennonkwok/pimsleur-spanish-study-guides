## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. Giving directions: *doblar* + direction + *en el semáforo***
*Doblar* (to turn) takes directional phrases and often a landmark.

- *Doble a la derecha en el semáforo.* — Turn to the right at the traffic light.
- *Doble a la izquierda en el semáforo.* — Turn to the left at the traffic light.
- *Y después siga derecho tres cuadras.* — And then continue straight ahead three blocks.

**2. *lucir* — to look / to appear**
Used to describe how someone looks or appears, especially regarding health, mood, or appearance.

- *Luce cansada.* — You look tired. (addressing a woman)
- *Usted luce bien.* — You look well.
- *Luce cansado.* — You look tired. (addressing a man)

**3. Plural possessive pronouns: *míos/mías*, *suyos/suyas***
When the possessed noun is plural, the possessive pronoun must also be plural.

| Pronoun | Masculine pl. | Feminine pl. |
|---|---|---|
| mine | *míos* | *mías* |
| yours/his/hers/theirs | *suyos* | *suyas* |

- *¿Son suyos?* — Are they yours? (referring to *anteojos de sol*, masculine plural)
- *Sí, son míos.* — Yes, they're mine.

**4. *quisiera* — polite wish / conditional ("I would like / I wish")**
*Quisiera* is the imperfect subjunctive of *querer*, used as a very polite "would like" or to express wishes.

- *Quisiera estar de vacaciones.* — I wish I were on vacation. / I'd like to be on vacation.
- *Quisiera que estuviera aquí.* — I wish she were here. (*que* + imperfect subjunctive)
- *Quisiera que estuviera aquí conmigo.* — I wish she were here with me.

**5. *me pregunto si* + future — "I wonder if..."**
*Preguntarse* (to wonder/ask oneself) + *si* (if) + future tense expresses wondering about a future event.

- *Me pregunto si vendrá.* — I wonder if she'll come.
- *vendrá* = future of *venir* (irregular: *vendr-* + future endings)

**6. *¿puedo ayudarle en algo?* — standard sales clerk greeting**
Lit. "Can I help you with something?" — common commercial Spanish phrase.

## Verb Conjugations

### doblar — to turn
**Type:** regular -ar

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: doblo | nosotros: doblamos |
| 2nd | tú: doblas | vosotros: dobláis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: dobla | ellos/ellas/Uds.: doblan |

Lesson drills: *doble a la derecha en el semáforo*, *doble a la izquierda en el semáforo* (Ud. imperative).

---

### lucir — to look / to appear
**Type:** irregular in yo present (*luzco*); otherwise regular -ir spelling

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **luzco** | nosotros: lucimos |
| 2nd | tú: luces | vosotros: lucís |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: **luce** | ellos/ellas/Uds.: lucen |

Lesson drills: *luce cansada*, *luce bien*, *usted luce bien*.

---

### pedir — to order / to ask for
**Type:** stem-changing (e→i) in all present forms except nosotros/vosotros

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **pido** | nosotros: pedimos |
| 2nd | tú: **pides** | vosotros: pedís |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: **pide** | ellos/ellas/Uds.: **piden** |

Lesson drills: *voy a pedir el flan*.

---

### venir — to come (future tense introduced)
**Type:** irregular; future stem *vendr-*

**Present tense** (review):

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: vengo | nosotros: venimos |
| 2nd | tú: vienes | vosotros: venís |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: viene | ellos/ellas/Uds.: vienen |

**Future tense** (new in this lesson):

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: vendré | nosotros: vendremos |
| 2nd | tú: vendrás | vosotros: vendréis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: **vendrá** | ellos/ellas/Uds.: vendrán |

Lesson drills: *me pregunto si vendrá* (3rd sg. future: will she come?).

---

## Summary

- **doblar** — regular -ar; directions verb; *doble a la derecha/izquierda en el semáforo*
- **lucir** — irregular yo (*luzco*); used for appearance: *luce bien*, *luce cansada*
- **pedir** — stem-changing (e→i); used for ordering food: *voy a pedir el flan*
- **venir** — irregular; future stem *vendr-*; *vendrá* introduced in *me pregunto si vendrá* construction
