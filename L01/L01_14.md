## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. Telling time: *son las…* vs. *es la una***
In Spanish, clock times use *ser* and treat the hours as a plural noun:
- *Son las tres.* — It's three o'clock. (Lit.: They are the three.)
- *Son las cinco y cuarto.* — It's five fifteen.
- Exception: *Es la una.* — It's one o'clock. (singular)

**2. Quarter-to time: *menos cuarto***
- *A las cinco menos cuarto.* — At quarter to five (= 4:45)
- *A las diez menos cuarto.* — At quarter to ten (= 9:45)
- Contrast: *a las diez y cuarto* = at 10:15 (quarter past)

**3. *Más tarde* (later)**
- *Más* = more; *tarde* = late → together = later
- *Quiero hablar más tarde.* — I want to talk later.
- *Ahora no. Pero más tarde.* — Not now. But later.

**4. Duration with *en* (in [amount of time])**
- *En una hora.* — In one hour.
- *En una hora y media.* — In an hour and a half.
- *En dos horas.* — In two hours.

**5. Numbers: the 20s contract**
- *veinte* (20) contracts to *veinti-* when combined: *veintiuno, veinticinco*
- The 30s and 40s use *y*: *treinta y cinco, cuarenta y dos*

### Formal vs. Informal
Lesson 14 uses both registers:
- Opening dialogue: **informal** (*tomas*, *puedes*, *comes*)
- Hotel concierge interaction: **formal** (*usted*: *Son las cinco, señor*)
- *Nos vemos mañana* is register-neutral.

### Pronunciation Notes
- *Dormir*: DOOR-meer — the *o* is full, not reduced.
- *Temprano*: tem-PRA-no — stress on second syllable.
- *Menos cuarto*: ME-nos KWAR-to — spoken quickly, the words blend.
- *Son las tres*: the *s* in *son* and *las* link to the following vowel; sounds like *so-NLA-stres*.
- *Veinticinco*: veyn-ti-SEEN-ko — one word, no pause between *veinti* and *cinco*.

## Summary
- **dormir** — stem-changing (o→ue); nosotros/vosotros keep *o*
- **hablar** — regular -ar (to speak/talk)
- **estar** — irregular; used for states, locations, and conditions
- **ser** — irregular; used for permanent traits and telling time
- **tomar** — regular -ar (to take/drink/have)
- **comer** — regular -er (to eat)
- **querer** — stem-changing (e→ie); nosotros/vosotros keep *e*
- **poder** — stem-changing (o→ue); nosotros/vosotros keep *o*
- **ir** — irregular; memorize all 6 forms
