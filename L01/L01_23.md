## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *Conocer* — knowing a person or place (vs. *saber* — knowing a fact)**
*Conocer* is used for personal acquaintance or familiarity with a place. It requires the personal *a* before a specific person.

- *¿Conoces a un buen médico?* — Do you know a good doctor?
- *Conozco a un buen médico.* — I know a good doctor.
- *Conozco a la doctora Rodríguez.* — I know Dr. Rodríguez.

Irregular yo form: *conozco*. All other present forms are regular.

**2. Direct object pronouns: *lo* and *la***
Replace a previously mentioned noun to avoid repetition:

- *¿Quieres su número? Sí, lo necesito.* — Do you want his number? Yes, I need it. (*lo* replaces *el número* — masc.)
- *Escríbelo.* — Write it down. (*lo* attached to tú command of *escribir*)

**3. *Tener que* + infinitive — obligation**
- *Tienes que hablar despacio.* — You have to speak slowly.
- Construction: *tener* (conjugated) + *que* + infinitive.

**4. *Dime* — informal command of *decir***
The tú command of *decir* (to say/tell) is irregular: *di*. With the indirect pronoun *me* attached: *dime* (tell me).

**5. Comparatives with *más … que***
- *Más rápido que tú.* — Faster than you.
- Pattern: *más* + adjective/adverb + *que* + second item.

**6. Object pronoun + *recordar***
- *No lo recuerdo.* — I don't remember it. (*lo* = the number, masc.)
- Alternate: *No me acuerdo.* (reflexive *acordarse*)

### Formal vs. Informal
Lesson 23 drills primarily **informal** (*tú*) register: *conoces*, *quieres*, *tienes*, *crees*. The supplement dialogue and doctor context introduce formal address (*usted*) forms.

### Pronunciation Notes
- *Escríbelo*: stress on the third syllable — es-CRÍ-be-lo. The accent mark is required because adding the pronoun shifts the syllable count.
- *Médico*: stress on the first syllable — MÉ-di-co (esdrújula).
- *Conozco*: the *z* before *c* produces a /s/ sound in Latin American Spanish.

## Verb Conjugations

### conocer — to know (a person/place)
**Type:** irregular yo (*conozco*); all other forms regular -er

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **conozco** | nosotros: conocemos |
| 2nd | tú: conoces | vosotros: conocéis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: conoce | ellos/ellas/Uds.: conocen |

Lesson 23 drills: *conozco* (I know), *conoces* (do you know?), *conocen* (they know).

- **conocer** — irregular yo *conozco*; otherwise regular -er
- **querer** — stem-changing (e→ie); irregular yo *quiero*
- **creer** — regular -er
- **estar** — irregular; memorize all 6 forms
- **tener** — irregular; yo *tengo*; tú *tienes* (e→ie)
- **necesitar** — regular -ar
- **recordar** — stem-changing (o→ue)
- **tomar** — regular -ar
- **escribir** — regular -ir
