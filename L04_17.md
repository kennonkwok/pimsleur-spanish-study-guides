## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *Reservar* with direct object pronoun: *reservarla***
When a noun (like *habitación*) has already been mentioned, Spanish replaces it with a direct object pronoun attached to the infinitive:

- *Quiero reservar una habitación.* — I want to reserve a room.
- *Quiero reservarla para tres días.* — I want to reserve it for three days.

The feminine pronoun *la* agrees with *habitación* (feminine).

**2. Hotel check-in phrases**
Key pattern for checking into a hotel:

- *Reservé una habitación para cuatro días.* — I reserved a room for four days.
- *Para esta noche hasta el jueves, inclusive.* — For tonight until Thursday, inclusive.
- *Tome su llave.* — Take your key. (formal command)
- *Necesita ayuda con su equipaje?* — Do you need help with your luggage?

**3. *El del medio* — the middle one**
Used to describe the child who is neither oldest nor youngest:

- *Juan es el del medio.* — Juan is the middle one.
- Contrast: *el mayor* (oldest) / *el del medio* (middle) / *el menor* (youngest)

**4. *Revisar* — to look through / check**
New verb meaning to review/check (documents, messages):

- *Necesito revisar mis mensajes electrónicos.* — I need to look through my email messages.
- *Tengo que revisar mis mensajes.* — I have to check my messages.

**5. *Tantos/as* — so many**
Agreement with noun in gender and number:

- *Compré tantas cosas.* — I bought so many things. (cosas = f pl)
- *Son muchos regalos.* — They are many gifts.

**6. Date ranges in hotel context**
- *De [day] al [day] inclusive.* — From [day] through [day] inclusive.
- *Del martes al jueves inclusive.* — From Tuesday through Thursday inclusive.
- *Para esta noche hasta el jueves.* — For tonight until Thursday.

## Verb Conjugations

### revisar — to check / look through
**Type:** regular -ar

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: reviso | nosotros: revisamos |
| 2nd | tú: revisas | vosotros: revisáis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: revisa | ellos/ellas/Uds.: revisan |

Lesson drills: *necesito revisar mis mensajes electrónicos*, *tengo que revisar mis mensajes*

---

### vender — to sell

| | Singular | Plural |

| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: vende | ellos/ellas/Uds.: **venden** |

## Summary

- **revisar** — regular -ar; means to check/look through; *necesito revisar mis mensajes*
- **vender** — regular -er; introduced for shopping: *¿dónde venden equipaje?*
- **quedarse** — reflexive -ar; *¿dónde se está quedando?* = where are you staying?
- Key grammar: direct object pronoun attached to infinitive (*reservarla*); date range pattern *de [day] al [day] inclusive*; *el del medio* = the middle child
