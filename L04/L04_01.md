## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *desde hace* + time — duration of ongoing action**
Used to express how long something has been going on (and still is). Equivalent to English "for [X time]."

- *Vivo allí desde hace 20 años.* — I've lived there for 20 years.
- *Soy programador desde hace cinco años.* — I've been a programmer for five years.

The present tense is used in Spanish (not a perfect tense) because the action is still ongoing.

**2. *perdón por* + infinitive — apologizing for an action**
*Por* + infinitive creates a phrase meaning "for [doing something]."

- *Perdón por llegar tarde.* — Excuse me for arriving late.
- *Gracias por la despedida.* — Thank you for the farewell.

**3. Preterite of *decir* (to say)**
*Decir* has an irregular preterite stem *dij-*:

- *dije* (I said), *dijiste* (you said), *dijo* (he/she said)
- *¿Qué dijo?* — What did he/she say?
- *Dije que soy programador de software.* — I said that I'm a software programmer.

**4. Preterite of *ir* (to go)**
*Ir* in the preterite is irregular: *fui, fuiste, fue, fuimos, fuisteis, fueron*. It shares the same preterite forms as *ser*.

- *Fui a un congreso.* — I went to a conference.

**5. *ir a* + infinitive — near future**
Revisited at Level 4 with more complex infinitive phrases:

- *Voy a reunirme con un cliente.* — I'm going to meet with a client.
- *Vamos a reunirnos mañana.* — We're going to meet tomorrow.

**6. *demasiado* + adjective — too [adjective]**

- *Son demasiado grandes para jugar con juguetes.* — They are too big for playing with toys.

## Verb Conjugations

### viajar — to travel
**Type:** regular -ar

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **viajo** | nosotros: viajamos |
| 2nd | tú: viajas | vosotros: viajáis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: viaja | ellos/ellas/Uds.: viajan |

Lesson drills: *viajo a la Ciudad de México*, *viaja a México*.

---

### escribir — to write
**Type:** regular -ir

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **escribo** | nosotros: escribimos |
| 2nd | tú: escribes | vosotros: escribís |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: escribe | ellos/ellas/Uds.: escriben |

Lesson drills: *escribo software de negocios*, *escribe software de negocios*.

---

### reunirse — to meet with / to get together
**Type:** regular -ir (reflexive)

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **me reúno** | nosotros: nos reunimos |
| 2nd | tú: te reúnes | vosotros: os reunís |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: se reúne | ellos/ellas/Uds.: se reúnen |

Lesson drills: *voy a reunirme con un cliente*, *voy a reunirme con una conocida*. Note: in the lesson the verb appears primarily in *ir a* + infinitive constructions (*reunirme*, *reunirse*).

---

### decir — to say / to tell
**Type:** irregular (*go*-verb in yo; e→i stem-changing; irregular preterite)

Present tense:

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **digo** | nosotros: decimos |
| 2nd | tú: **dices** | vosotros: decís |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: **dice** | ellos/ellas/Uds.: **dicen** |

Preterite (drilled forms):

| yo | tú | él/ella | nosotros | vosotros | ellos |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| dije | dijiste | **dijo** | dijimos | dijisteis | dijeron |

Lesson drills: *¿qué dijo?*, *dije que soy programador*.

## Summary

- **viajar** — regular -ar; core travel verb for this level
- **escribir** — regular -ir; "I write business software" is a key identity phrase drilled throughout
- **reunirse** — regular -ir reflexive; *reunirse con* = to meet with; appears mainly as *voy a reunirme con...*
- **decir** — irregular; preterite *dije/dijo* introduced for reporting speech
