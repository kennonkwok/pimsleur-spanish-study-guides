## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *Me han dicho que…* — They have told me that…**
Uses the present perfect of *decir* (*han dicho*) plus *que* to report what was said:
- *Me han dicho que tú te vas de viaje.* — They've told me that you're going on a trip.

**2. *irse de viaje / irse de vacaciones***
*Irse* (reflexive of *ir*) expresses departure or leaving a place:
- *Te vas de viaje.* — You're going on a trip.
- *Estoy de vacaciones.* — I'm on vacation.

**3. *saber* vs. *conocer***
Both mean "to know," but used differently:
| *saber* | *conocer* |
|---|---|
| Facts, how to do something | People, places, things (familiarity) |
| *No sé nada de ingeniería.* | *Conozco ese libro.* |

**4. *llover* — impersonal stem-changing verb**
*Llover* (o→ue) is used only in 3rd person singular because it's impersonal (the subject is weather):
- *Llueve.* — It's raining.
- *Si llueve…* — If it rains…
- *La lluvia ha parado.* — The rain has stopped.

**5. Conditional: *podría* and *podrías***
Used for polite suggestions or hypothetical ability:
- *Quizá yo podría esperar.* — Maybe I could wait.
- *Quizá podrías ir con nosotros.* — Maybe you could go with us.

**6. Imperative with object pronoun — *Toma tus libros***
The informal imperative of *tomar* (tú form) is *toma*:
- *Toma tus libros.* — Take your books.
- *Toma tus libros de ingeniería.* — Take your engineering books.

**7. *creo que* — I think / I believe**
- *Creo que la lluvia ha parado por hoy.* — I think the rain has stopped for today.

## Verb Conjugations

### saber — to know (facts)
**Type:** irregular (*yo* form: *sé*; subjunctive base: *sep-*)

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **sé** | nosotros: sabemos |
| 2nd | tú: sabes | vosotros: sabéis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: sabe | ellos/ellas/Uds.: saben |

Lesson drills: *no sé nada de ingeniería*.

## Summary
- **saber** — irregular (*sé*); to know facts; *no sé nada* = I know nothing
- **conocer** — irregular (*conozco*); to know people/places
- **llover** — impersonal stem-changing (o→ue); only 3rd sg. *llueve* used
- **llamar** — regular -ar; "to call"; *llamarme/llamarte* with attached clitics
- **funcionar** — regular -ar; "to work/function" (machines)
- **estudiar** — regular -ar; "to study"
