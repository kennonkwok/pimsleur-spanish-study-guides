## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. Informal direction imperatives: *ve, dobla, camina, sigue, no dobles***
A set of informal (tú) imperative forms for giving directions:

| Action | Informal (tú) | Formal (Ud.) |
|---|---|---|
| Go | *ve* | *vaya* |
| Turn | *dobla* | *doble* |
| Walk | *camina* | *camine* |
| Go straight | *sigue derecho* | *siga derecho* |
| Don't turn | *no dobles* | *no doble* |

- *Ve hasta el semáforo, luego dobla a la izquierda.* — Go to the traffic light, then turn left.
- Negative informal imperative uses present subjunctive: *dobla* → *no dobles*

**2. *sigue derecho* — *seguir* informal imperative**
*Seguir* (e→i, irregular) for "go straight / continue":

- *Sigue derecho dos cuadras más.* — Keep going straight for two more blocks.
- *Sigue hasta la biblioteca.* — Go straight to the library.
- Formal: *siga derecho*

**3. *ni... ni...* — "neither... nor..."**
Double negative with *ni* is correct in Spanish (no double negative error):

- *No dobles ni a la derecha ni a la izquierda.* — Don't turn either right or left.
- *No tengo ni dinero ni tarjeta.* — I have neither money nor a card.
- If *ni* precedes the verb, *no* is optional: *Ni a la derecha ni a la izquierda* (same meaning)

**4. *puedo dárselos* — IO *le* → *se* before DO *lo/la/los/las***
When an indirect object pronoun (*le/les*) precedes a direct object pronoun (*lo/la/los/las*), *le/les* becomes *se*:

| Without change (wrong) | Correct form | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| *Le los doy* | ***Se** los doy* | I give them to him/her/you |
| *Le la doy* | ***Se** la doy* | I give it to him/her/you |

- *Si necesita pasta y cepillo, puedo dárselos.* — If you need toothpaste and a toothbrush, I can give them to you.
- *dárselos* = *dar* + *se* (IO, replaces *le*) + *los* (DO)

**5. *avíseme* — "please let me know" (formal imperative)**
*Avisar* (to notify/advise) in the formal imperative + *me*:

- *Avíseme si va a llegar tarde.* — Please let me know if you're going to arrive late.
- *Avísame* (informal): *Avísame si llegas tarde.*

**6. *apague la luz* — *apagar* formal imperative (g→gu)**
*Apagar* (to turn off) has a spelling change in subjunctive/formal imperative:

- *apagar* → *apague* (g → gu before *e*): *Apague la luz.* — Turn off the light.
- *apaga* (informal): *Apaga la luz, por favor.*
- *al salir de la habitación* = upon leaving the room (reviewed)

## Verb Conjugations

### reciclar — to recycle
**Type:** regular -ar

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: reciclo | nosotros: reciclamos |
| 2nd | tú: reciclas | vosotros: recicláis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: recicla | ellos/ellas/Uds.: reciclan |

Lesson drills: *la gente recicla mucho*, *no reciclan en todas partes de Perú*.

---

### apagar — to turn off
**Type:** regular -ar with spelling change g→gu before e/i

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: apago | nosotros: apagamos |
| 2nd | tú: apaga | vosotros: apagáis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: apaga | ellos/ellas/Uds.: apagan |

Formal imperative: ***apague*** (Ud.); informal: *apaga* (tú)
Lesson drills: *por favor, apague la luz al salir de la habitación*.

---

### seguir — to continue / go straight
**Type:** stem-changing (e→i); irregular yo (*sigo*)

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **sigo** | nosotros: seguimos |
| 2nd | tú: **sigues** | vosotros: seguís |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: **sigue** | ellos/ellas/Uds.: **siguen** |

Informal imperative: ***sigue*** (tú); formal: *siga* (Ud.)
Lesson drills: *sigue derecho dos cuadras*, *siga derecho hasta la biblioteca*.

## Summary

- **reciclar** — regular -ar; *reciclan mucho* = they recycle a lot
- **apagar** — regular -ar (g→gu); formal imp. *apague*; *apague la luz* = turn off the light
- **seguir** — e→i; informal imp. *sigue derecho* = go straight; formal *siga*
- ***puedo dárselos*** — IO *le* → *se* before DO *los*: I can give them to you
- ***ni A ni B*** = neither A nor B; *no dobles ni a la derecha ni a la izquierda*
- ***avíseme*** = please let me know (formal); *avisar* = to notify
- ***bienvenido/a/os*** = welcome; agrees in gender/number with the person(s) greeted
- ***el cambio climático*** = climate change; ***el medio ambiente*** = the environment
