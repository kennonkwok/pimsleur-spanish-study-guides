## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *estar* with health and weather conditions**
*Estar* is used for temporary states, including sickness and weather conditions:

- *Mi hija está enferma.* — My daughter is sick.
- *Está soleado.* — It's sunny.
- *No está tan enferma.* — She's not that sick.

Compare weather with *hacer* (temperature) vs. *estar* (sky/conditions):
- *Hace calor.* — It's hot. (temperature)
- *Está soleado.* — It's sunny. (sky condition)
- *Hace calor y está soleado.* — It's hot and sunny.

**2. *otros/otras* — "other" (adjective/pronoun)**
*Otros* agrees in gender and number with the noun it modifies:

- *Mis otros hijos.* — My other children. (m pl)
- *Mis otras hijas.* — My other daughters. (f pl)
- *Otro día.* — Another day. (m sg)

**3. *pasar* — "to happen"; *¿qué pasa?***
*Pasar* is a regular -ar verb used impersonally:

- *¿Qué pasa?* — What's happening?
- *A veces pasa eso.* — Sometimes that happens.
- *No sé qué pasa.* — I don't know what's happening.

**4. Object pronouns attached to infinitives: *leerlos*, *llevarla*, *encontrarla***
Object pronouns can be attached to infinitives. The pronoun agrees with the gender/number of the object:

| Pronoun | Refers to | Example |
|---|---|---|
| *-la* | feminine singular | *Voy a llevarla al médico.* — I'm going to take her to the doctor. |
| *-la* | feminine singular | *Puedo encontrarla.* — I can find it (the address, f). |
| *-los* | masculine plural | *Tengo que leerlos.* — I have to read them (the papers, m pl). |

**5. *este / estos* — "this / these" (demonstrative adjectives)**
The lesson introduces demonstratives:

- *Este papel.* — This paper. (m sg)
- *Este documento.* — This document. (m sg)
- *Estos documentos.* — These documents. (m pl)

*Esta* (f sg) / *estas* (f pl) for feminine nouns: *esta tarde*, *estas cosas*.

**6. *menos* as "except"**
*Menos* can mean "except" as well as "less":

- *Todos bien, menos mi hija pequeña.* — Everyone fine, except my little daughter.

## Verb Conjugations

### pasar — to happen / to pass
**Type:** regular -ar

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: paso | nosotros: pasamos |
| 2nd | tú: pasas | vosotros: pasáis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: **pasa** | ellos/ellas/Uds.: pasan |

Lesson drills: *¿qué pasa?*, *a veces pasa eso*, *no sé qué pasa*.
