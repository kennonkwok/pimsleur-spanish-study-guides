## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *estoy tratando de* + infinitive — I'm trying to**
*Tratar de* + infinitive = "to try to." With *estar* + gerund it becomes progressive:
- *Estoy tratando de encontrarlos.* — I'm trying to find them.
- *Estoy tratando de encontrarla.* — I'm trying to find it (f).
- *Estoy tratando de encontrar.* — I'm trying to find [it].

**2. *hace* + time expression = "ago"**
- *Lo tuve hace unas semanas.* — I had it a few weeks ago.
- *Hace un mes.* — A month ago.

**3. *mostrar* — to show (formal vs. informal)**
*Mostrar* (o→ue) takes indirect object pronouns to indicate who is shown:
- *¿Puede mostrarme?* — Can you show me? (formal)
- *Puedo mostrarle.* — I can show you. (formal, speaker → listener)
- *Voy a mostrarte el trabajo.* — I'm going to show you the work. (informal)

**4. Formal imperative with -ar verbs: *gire*, *déjeme*, *tome***
For formal (*usted*) commands, -ar verbs use -e ending:
- *Gire a la izquierda.* — Turn to the left.
- *Déjeme ver.* — Let me see.

**5. *mil* — thousand**
- *Mil días.* — A thousand days.
- *Tres mil pesos.* — Three thousand pesos.
- *Dos mil.* — Two thousand.
Note: *mil* is invariable (no *un mil*; never pluralized with numbers).

**6. *todo junto* — all together**
- *Todo junto, dos mil.* — All together, two thousand.
- *Todo junto, dos mil pesos.* — All together, two thousand pesos.

**7. *demasiado* as adjective — agrees in gender/number**
- *Tenemos demasiadas reuniones.* — We have too many meetings.
- *Demasiado trabajo.* — Too much work.

**8. *ya regreso* — I'll be right back**
*Ya* (already) + *regresar* in present tense creates this idiomatic expression:
- *Ya regreso.* — I'll be right back. (Lit. "Already I return.")

## Verb Conjugations

### encontrar — to find
**Type:** stem-changing (o→ue)

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **encuentro** | nosotros: encontramos |
| 2nd | tú: **encuentras** | vosotros: encontráis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: **encuentra** | ellos/ellas/Uds.: **encuentran** |

Lesson drills: *estoy tratando de encontrarlos*, *estoy tratando de encontrarla*.

---

## Summary
- **encontrar** — stem-changing (o→ue); "to find"; often used with *tratando de*
- **mostrar** — stem-changing (o→ue); "to show"; *mostrarme/mostrarle/mostrarte* with clitics
- **parar** — regular -ar; "to stop"; drilled in present perfect (*ha parado*)
- **buscar** — regular -ar; "to look for" (no preposition)
- **decir** — irregular (*digo*, preterite *dije*); "to say/tell"
