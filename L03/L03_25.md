## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *hay que* + infinitive — impersonal obligation**
*Hay que* expresses obligation without specifying who must do it ("one must / you have to"). The infinitive never changes:

- *Hay que llenar unos formularios.* — One must fill out some forms.
- *¿Cuántos formularios hay que llenar?* — How many forms must one fill out?

Compare with *tener que* (personal obligation): *Tengo que firmar.* = I have to sign.

**2. *estaba* — imperfect of *estar* for past states**
The imperfect *estaba* expresses a state that existed in the past (not a completed action):

- *No estaba listo.* — It wasn't ready. (state that existed when I arrived)
- *Cuando fui a recoger el traje, no estaba listo.* — When I went to pick up the suit, it wasn't ready.

*Fui* (preterite) = completed action of going; *estaba* (imperfect) = ongoing state of not being ready.

**3. *podría* — conditional of *poder* ("could")**
The conditional of *poder* expresses hypothetical ability:

- *Podría trabajar en una empresa grande.* — He could work at a big company.
- *¿Dónde debería firmar?* — Where should I sign?

Both are conditional forms, expressing what could or should happen.

**4. *se me olvidó pedir* — chaining *se me olvidó* with an infinitive**
The involuntary *se* construction can take an infinitive as its subject:

- *Se me olvidó pedir el servicio extra rápido.* — I forgot to ask for the express service. (Lit. "Asking for express service forgot itself to me.")
- *Se me olvidó mi celular también.* — I also forgot my cell phone.

**5. *cuando fui a* + infinitive — "when I went to [do something]"**
*Cuando* + preterite sets a completed past time reference:

- *Cuando fui a recoger el traje.* — When I went to pick up the suit.
- *Cuando fui a la tintorería.* — When I went to the dry cleaner.

## Verb Conjugations

### encontrar — to find
**Type:** stem-changing (o→ue) — stem changes in all forms except nosotros/vosotros

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **encuentro** | nosotros: encontramos |
| 2nd | tú: **encuentras** | vosotros: encontráis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: **encuentra** | ellos/ellas/Uds.: **encuentran** |

Lesson drills: *no puedo encontrar mi tarjeta*.

---

## Summary

- **encontrar** — stem-changing (o→ue); *no puedo encontrar mi tarjeta*
- **llenar** — regular -ar; *llenar un formulario* = to fill out a form
- **firmar** — regular -ar; *tiene que firmar en la línea*
- **pedir** — stem-changing (e→i); *se me olvidó pedir el servicio extra rápido*
- **conseguir** — stem-changing (e→i) + *go* yo (*consigo*); *conseguir un trabajo* = to get a job
