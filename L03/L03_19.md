## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *me fue* — how something went for you (indirect object + *fue*)**
The construction *¿cómo te/le/me fue?* uses the preterite of *ir* with an indirect object pronoun to ask/say how an event went for someone.

- *¿Cómo te fue en el viaje?* — How did the trip go for you?
- *Me fue muy bien.* — It went very well for me.
- *Me fue mal.* — It went badly for me.

**2. *hay que* + infinitive (impersonal obligation)**
*Hay que* expresses an obligation without specifying who must do it. The verb is always *hay* (3rd-person singular) and is followed by an infinitive.

- *Hay que reparar mi carro.* — My car needs to be repaired. / One must repair my car.
- Compare with *tener que* (specific person): *Tengo que reparar mi carro.*

**3. Speed and distance expressions**
- *¿A cuántos kilómetros por hora?* — At how many kilometers per hour?
- *¿A cuántas millas por hora?* — At how many miles per hour?
- Note the gender agreement: *kilómetros* → *cuántos* (m pl); *millas* → *cuántas* (f pl)

**4. *llevar* + indirect object pronoun for giving rides**
*Llevar* (to carry / take) is used to offer or request a ride:

- *¿Puedes llevarme a la calle California?* — Can you take me to California Street?
- *No puedo llevarte.* — I can't take you.

## Verb Conjugations

### conseguir — to get / obtain
**Type:** stem-changing (e→i), irregular *yo* form; also *g* insert in *yo*

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **consigo** | nosotros: conseguimos |
| 2nd | tú: **consigues** | vosotros: conseguís |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: **consigue** | ellos/ellas/Uds.: **consiguen** |

Lesson drills: *quiero conseguir este ascenso*, *mi hermano acaba de conseguir un ascenso*.

---

## Summary

- **conseguir** — stem-changing (e→i), irregular *yo* (*consigo*); used with *ascenso* for job promotions
- **manejar** — regular -ar; Latin American verb for driving (Spain: *conducir*)
- **llevar** — regular -ar; extends to "giving a ride" with indirect object pronouns
- **reparar** — regular -ar; used in impersonal *hay que* construction
