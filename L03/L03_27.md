## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *estaban* — imperfect of *estar* for past location/state**
*Estaban* (3rd person plural imperfect of *estar*) describes where things were or a state that existed in the past:

- *Los materiales estaban por aquí.* — The materials were around here.
- *Estaban por aquí hace poco.* — They were around here a little while ago.
- Compare preterite *estuvieron* (completed past) vs. imperfect *estaban* (ongoing/background state).

**2. *hace poco* — "a little while ago"**
*Hace* + time expression indicates how long ago something happened:

- *Hace poco* — a little while ago
- *Hace dos días* — two days ago
- *Hace un mes* — a month ago

**3. *se puede* + infinitive — impersonal "one can"**
*Se puede* is the impersonal/passive construction meaning "it is possible to / one can":

- *¿Se puede cambiar dinero aquí?* — Can one exchange money here?
- *Se puede cambiar dinero aquí.* — One can exchange money here.
- *Se puede estacionar atrás.* — One can park in the back.
- *Se puede ver el parque en un día.* — One can see the park in a day.

Compare *puedo* (I can) vs. *se puede* (one can / it's possible).

**4. *dejar* + object + adjective — "to leave [something] [in a state]"**
*Dejar* + direct object + adjective expresses leaving something in a particular condition:

- *Quiero dejar todo listo.* — I want to leave everything ready.
- *Quiero dejar todo bien organizado.* — I want to leave everything well organized.

**5. *del [date] al [date]* — date ranges**
To express a span of time between two dates:

- *La exposición es del 8 al 10.* — The trade show is from the 8th to the 10th.
- *Del* = *de* + *el* (contracted)

**6. *antes de irme/irte* — "before I/you leave"**
*Antes de* + reflexive infinitive of *irse*:

- *Antes de irme, quiero dejar todo listo.* — Before I leave, I want to leave everything ready.
- *Antes de irte.* — Before you leave.

## Verb Conjugations

### organizar — to organize
**Type:** regular -ar (spelling change z→c before e/i: subjunctive *organice*)

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: organizo | nosotros: organizamos |
| 2nd | tú: organizas | vosotros: organizáis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: **organiza** | ellos/ellas/Uds.: organizan |

Lesson drills: *bien organizado* (adj. form), *¿está bien organizado?*, *es una persona muy organizada*, *quiero dejar todo bien organizado*.

## Summary

- **organizar** — regular -ar (z→c before e); *bien organizado* = well organized
- **estar** — irregular; imperfect *estaban* = they were (past location/state); drilled heavily: *estaban por aquí hace poco*
- **irse** — irregular reflexive; *me voy de vacaciones* = I'm going on vacation; *antes de irme*
- **estacionar** — regular -ar; *se puede estacionar* = one can park
