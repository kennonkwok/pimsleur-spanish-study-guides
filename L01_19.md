## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *Todos* — all / everyone**
*Todos* (masculine or mixed group) and *todas* (feminine group) function as both adjective and pronoun.
- *Todos van a comer juntos.* — Everyone is going to eat together.
- *Vamos todos.* — Let's all go. / We're all going.
- *Todos nos vamos.* — We're all leaving.

**2. *De vacaciones* — on vacation**
The phrase *de vacaciones* is fixed and always plural. Combine with departure verbs:
- *Me voy de vacaciones.* — I'm going on vacation.
- *Te vas de vacaciones.* — You're going on vacation.
- *Se van de vacaciones en dos semanas.* — They're going on vacation in two weeks.

**3. Time expressions: *en + time period***
Use *en* to say "in [amount of time]":
- *en una semana* — in one week
- *en dos semanas* — in two weeks
- *en un mes* — in one month
- *en dos meses* — in two months

**4. *Del restaurante* — from the restaurant**
*De + el* always contracts to *del*. This contraction is mandatory in Spanish.
- *Nos vamos del restaurante.* — We're leaving the restaurant.
- *Me voy del hotel.* — I'm leaving the hotel.

**5. *Nos vemos* — see you**
Reciprocal reflexive of *ver* (to see). Used as a farewell phrase, literally "we see each other."
- *Nos vemos a las tres de la tarde.* — See you at three in the afternoon.

**6. *¿Cuándo te vas / se van?* — When are you/they leaving?**
*¿Cuándo?* (when) combined with reflexive departure forms:
- *¿Cuándo te vas de vacaciones?* — When are you leaving on vacation?
- *¿Cuándo se van?* — When are they leaving?

### Patterns Drilled

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| Express wanting to know | *Quiero saber.* / *Quieres saber más números.* |
| State going on vacation | *Me voy de vacaciones en una semana.* |
| Ask when someone is leaving | *¿Cuándo te vas de vacaciones?* |
| State leaving in X time | *Se van en dos semanas.* |
| Farewell with time | *Nos vemos a las tres de la tarde.* |
| Leave a place | *Nos vamos del restaurante.* |
| All-inclusive "let's go" | *Vamos todos.* |
| Everyone together | *Todos van a comer juntos.* |

### Formal vs. Informal
- *¿Cuándo te vas?* — informal (tú)
- *¿Cuándo se va usted?* — formal (usted)
- *¿Cuándo se van?* — plural (ustedes / ellos)

### Pronunciation Notes
- *Vacaciones*: five syllables — va-ca-CIONES; the *io* is a diphthong, treated as one syllable.
- *Semana*: stress on middle syllable — se-MA-na.
- *Del*: single syllable contraction; never written as *de el*.
- *Todos*: stress on first syllable — TO-dos.
- *Nos vemos*: run together in speech — "nosvemos."

## Verb Conjugations

### saber — to know (a fact or how)
**Type:** irregular (*yo* form); otherwise regular -er

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **sé** | nosotros: sabemos |
| 2nd | tú: sabes | vosotros: sabéis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: sabe | ellos/ellas/Uds.: saben |

Lesson 19 drills: *quiero saber* (I want to know), *quieres saber* (you want to know).

---

### querer — to want
**Type:** stem-changing (e→ie); irregular *yo* form

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **quiero** | nosotros: queremos |
| 2nd | tú: **quieres** | vosotros: queréis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: quiere | ellos/ellas/Uds.: quieren |

Lesson 19 drills: *quiero saber* (I want to know), *no quiero saber* (I don't want to know).

---

### ir — to go
**Type:** irregular

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: voy | nosotros: **vamos** |
| 2nd | tú: vas | vosotros: vais |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: va | ellos/ellas/Uds.: **van** |

Lesson 19 drills: *van a comer juntos* (they're going to eat together), *vamos todos* (let's all go).

Lesson 19 drills: *me voy de vacaciones*, *te vas de vacaciones*, *se va*, *se van*, *nos vamos del restaurante*.

## Summary
- **saber** — irregular *yo* (*sé*); otherwise regular -er; *quiero saber* = I want to know
- **querer** — stem-changing (e→ie); *quiero/quieres/quiere/quieren*
- **ir** — irregular; *vamos todos* = let's all go; *van* = they go
- **irse** — reflexive departure; *se va / se van* = he/she is leaving / they are leaving; *de vacaciones* always follows
- **tener** — irregular *yo* (*tengo*); e→ie stem change; *tienes / tiene*
