## Grammar Review

### New Concepts

**1. *¿A qué se dedica?* — asking what someone does for work**
This is the standard way to ask someone's profession in Spanish:

- *¿A qué se dedica usted?* — What do you do (for a living)?
- *Soy periodista.* — I'm a journalist.
- *Soy programadora de software.* — I'm a software programmer.

Note: professions do NOT take an article after *ser* when unmodified.

**2. *El mayor / la mayor* and *el menor / la menor* — eldest / youngest**
These are irregular comparatives used as nouns or adjectives:

| Form | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| *la mayor* | the oldest (f) | *La mayor vive en San Francisco.* |
| *el mayor* | the oldest (m) | *Él es el mayor de los tres.* |
| *la menor* | the youngest (f) | *María es la menor de las tres.* |
| *el menor* | the youngest (m) | *Bill es el menor.* |

The phrase *de los/las tres* specifies "of the three."

**3. *Una vez / dos veces* — frequency expressions**
- *una vez* = once / one time
- *dos veces* = twice / two times
- *por semana* = per week; *por mes* = per month

- *La llamo una vez o dos veces por semana.* — I call her once or twice a week.

**4. *Por lo general* — usually**
This adverb phrase modifies habitual actions:

- *Por lo general la llamo una vez por semana.* — Usually I call her once a week.
- *Por lo general envío mensajes electrónicos.* — I usually send email messages.

**5. Gender agreement with *programador/a***
Profession nouns that end in *-or* form the feminine by adding *-a*:

- *programador* → *programadora*
- *vendedor* → *vendedora*

## Verb Conjugations

### llamar — to call (by phone)
**Type:** regular -ar

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 1st | yo: **llamo** | nosotros: llamamos |
| 2nd | tú: llamas | vosotros: llamáis |
| 3rd | él/ella/Ud.: llama | ellos/ellas/Uds.: llaman |

Lesson drills: *la llamo una vez por semana*, *por lo general llamo*

## Summary

- **llamar** — regular -ar; key pattern: *la llamo una vez por semana*
- **buscar** — regular -ar; used both for shopping (*busco una bufanda*) and job searching (*buscando empleo*)
- **traer** — irregular (*traigo*); used in *¿puedo traerle...?* = can I bring you...?
- **enseñar** — regular -ar; appears in occupation context
- Key grammar: *el/la mayor / menor* for eldest/youngest; *¿a qué se dedica?* for profession; *por lo general* for habitual actions
